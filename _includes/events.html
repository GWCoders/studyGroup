Regular meetups

NOTE: Due to COVID-19 concerns, GW Coders will not be meeting until the University has returned to normal operations.

We meet every Friday at 11:00am in the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Lab at GWU (2000 Penn., 7th Floor) throughout the academic year. Light lunch is provided.

Unless otherwise stated, all our events are open to everyone (i.e. no registration necessary, just show up!). A list of all previous events and sessions can be found on the "Past Events" page.
